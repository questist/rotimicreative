Unmotive Podcast

Unmotive: The User Experience of Things is a podcast on technology that seeks to explore the design of the software products from by having professionals in the field talk about relevant topics to users. Hopefully, this will bridge the gap between professional and casual user. I got the idea from hearing about lines around the block for Apple's new iPhone and the generally accepted idea that Apples software and hardware design are top tier. I am hoping to explore this phenomenon more in depth through personal reflections and interviews.

Adapt This Blog

I worked with a 5 other writers to create a blog based on reviewing book to film adaptations. The blog was a personal not for profit site. It started with a more serious tone through the voices of it's authors. However, I found that adding some humor helped make the reviews easier to write and the content more cohesive especially on social media. I started including tongue in cheek references and memes. This was a way to pivot the sites content, currently I may release a similar project on that domain after some further research.
